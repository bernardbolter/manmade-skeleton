Team

Mike Grinser / Michael Kuhn

Engineer

Mike began his journey into music in the mid 90’s as an electronic music artist with countless releases and tours under his belt. After his studies in audio school Mike joined the MMM team in 2008. To date he has been mastering and cutting some of the most prolific electronic music labels in the industry. His meticulous and borderline perfectionist analog processing is sought out by many. His skill set as a cutting engineer is superior as he was initially taught how to transcribe music to master-disc on the vintage Scully/Westrex system, now on a fully modded Neumann VMS70. When Mike is not in the mastering studio he gives back to the audio community by teaching in depth audio classes at SAE audio school.

Tim Xavier Bockmier II

Engineer

Tim started his audio journey in the mid 90’s as a DJ, later moved onto producing 12” releases in Chicago ca. 2000 where he grew an insatiable appetite to become a mastering and cutting engineer. In 2004 Tim moved to Brooklyn New York where he spent the next three years doing his apprenticeship as a cutting engineer at Chopstick Dubplate. After a 3 year stint in Brooklyn, Tim eventually bought a Scully/Westrex cutting system and moved his entire operation to Berlin Germany under the new company name: Manmade Mastering. 13 years in the making Tim has built countless solid relationships with many respected electronic music outfits worldwide.
